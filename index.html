WHAT IS THE VENTURE
WORLD CUP ?
The Venture Cup is a global sporting event designed to bring the very best of motorsport, event spectacle, brands and consumers together in a new and exciting way. It’ll comprise over 1,000 miles of the very toughest conditions which the ocean can muster up, forcing teams to crash mercilessly through wave after relentless wave for hours on end.
True oshore racing is where technical excellence, bravery and courage will join forces to do battle against the elements. At the end of each day’s racing, the teams bring colour, spectacle and excitement to the festival villages along the route, allowing the public to actively participate in a major sporting event.
The world will be watching when the adventure begins in 2016, will you be part of it?
